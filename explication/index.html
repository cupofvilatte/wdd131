What Is Explication

You have learned that one of the principal tasks of philosophy is to make our fundamental ideas clear. But how do we do that? Philosophers have several tools for clarifying ideas, and you'll practice using these tools all semester. So, let's begin by learning what those tools are.

First, let's call the process of clarifying our ideas explication. To explicate an idea is to explain it in a systematic way so that we both clarify the idea and, at the same time, explore its meaning. The method of explication involves several intellectual moves or steps.
